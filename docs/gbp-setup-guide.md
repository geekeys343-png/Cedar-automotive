## RANKING FACTORS — What Actually Moves the Needle

1. **Proximity** (can't control) — Google shows businesses close to the searcher
2. **Relevance** (CAN control) — Categories, services, description, posts, Q&A
3. **Prominence** (CAN control) — Reviews, review velocity, photos, posts, citations

**Your levers:**
- More reviews = higher ranking (velocity matters more than total)
- More categories + services = more keyword matches
- More photos = more engagement signals
- More posts = more freshness signals
- Q&A = more keyword relevance
- Website link authority = citation building (next phase)

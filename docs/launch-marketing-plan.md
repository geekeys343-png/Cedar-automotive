## Executive Summary

Cedar Automotive is an independent auto repair shop in Cedar Rapids, IA that is already operational but needs a structured marketing push to build awareness and fill the schedule. The shop's key competitive advantages are:

1. **European vehicle expertise** — one of the few independent shops in the Cedar Rapids area equipped for BMW, Audi, Mercedes, and Volvo
2. **Airport proximity** — unique drop-off service for travelers
3. **Honest, no-BS pricing** — independent shop rates vs. dealership markups
4. **Full-service capability** — 11 service categories covering everything from oil changes to car key programming

This plan is organized into three phases over 90 days with clear action items, budget allocations, and success metrics.

## PHASE 2: Paid Acquisition (Weeks 3–6)
*Turn on the engines — capture demand*

### 2.1 Google Ads — Search Campaigns

**Budget:** $1,200/month ($40/day)

**Why Google Ads First:** People searching "auto repair cedar rapids" or "BMW mechanic near me" have immediate intent. This is the fastest path to booked appointments.

**Target Keywords:**
- auto repair cedar rapids
- mechanic cedar rapids
- car repair near me cedar rapids
- oil change cedar rapids
- brake repair cedar rapids
- check engine light cedar rapids
- auto shop near me (geo-targeted)

**Sample Ad:**
```
Cedar Automotive — Cedar Rapids
Honest Auto Repair | All Makes & Models
No upselling. Fair pricing. Same-day service available.
Near CR Airport. Call (319) 450-7584
```

**Ad Extensions to set up:**
- Location extension (from GBP)
- Call extension: (319) 450-7584
- Sitelink extensions: Services, About Us, Contact, Airport Drop-Off
- Callout extensions: Free Estimates, European Specialists, All Makes Welcome
- Structured snippets: Services — Oil Change, Brakes, Engine Diagnostics, Transmission

#### Campaign 2: European Vehicle Specialist ($400/mo)

**Target Keywords:**
- BMW repair cedar rapids

- Volvo service cedar rapids

Cedar Automotive — (319) 450-7584

**Why this campaign matters:** European vehicle owners often feel stuck paying dealership prices. The keyword "independent BMW mechanic" signals high intent and willingness to switch. This audience has higher average repair values.

  - "What are your hours?" — Mon–Fri 8 AM to 5 PM...

### 3.3 Review Generation Strategy

**Why:** Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor and the #1 trust signal for auto repair. Goal: 25+ reviews in first 90 days.

**Process:**
1. After every completed service, send a thank-you text/email
2. Include direct link to Google review page
3. Script: "Thanks for choosing Cedar Automotive! If you had a good experience, we'd appreciate a quick Google review — it really helps a small business like ours."

**Review Link Creation:**
- Go to GBP dashboard > "Ask for reviews" > copy the short link
- Use this link in all review requests

**Rules:**
- Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)
- Never ask for "5-star" reviews (ask for "honest" reviews)
- Respond to EVERY review (positive and negative) within 24 hours
- Keep responses professional, thankful, and brief

**Review Response Templates:**

*Positive review:*
> Thank you, [Name]! We're glad we could help with your [vehicle/service]. We appreciate you trusting us with your car — see you next time!

*Negative review:*
> Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. We take feedback seriously and would like to make this right. Please call us at (319) 450-7584 so we can discuss this directly.

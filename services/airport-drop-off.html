Auto Repair Near Cedar Rapids Airport (CID)

Cedar Automotive is conveniently located near Cedar Rapids Regional Airport (CID), and we've made it easy for travelers to get their car serviced without losing a single day. Drop your vehicle off at our shop before your flight, and pick it up fixed, serviced, and ready to go when you land. Zero trip disruption — your car gets expert care while you're away.

This service works for everything from routine maintenance like oil changes and brake service to more involved repairs like engine diagnostics, transmission work, or suspension repair. Just call us before your trip to schedule the work, drop your car off on the way to the airport, and we'll handle the rest. We'll call you with updates and have your vehicle ready by the time you return.

No need to take a day off work for car repairs. No need to sit in a waiting room. No need to arrange a ride. Our location near CID airport means you can combine your travel plans with your car maintenance — the most efficient way to get your vehicle serviced in Cedar Rapids.

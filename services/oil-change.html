Quality Oil Changes in Cedar Rapids

Regular oil changes are the single most important thing you can do to keep your engine running strong. At Cedar Automotive, we don't rush through oil changes — we use quality oil and filters matched to your vehicle's specifications, check your fluid levels, and give your car a basic visual inspection while it's on the lift.

We offer both conventional and full synthetic oil changes, as well as European-spec synthetic blends required by BMW, Audi, Mercedes, and Volvo. Many European vehicles require specific oil weight and certification standards that quick-lube shops can't always meet. We can.

Beyond oil changes, we handle all your vehicle's fluid needs. Coolant flush, brake fluid service, transmission fluid change, power steering fluid, and differential fluid — all the services that keep your car running smoothly through Iowa's hot summers and freezing winters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get an oil change?

It depends on your vehicle and oil type. Most modern cars using synthetic oil can go 5,000 to 7,500 miles between changes. We follow your manufacturer's recommendations for the best engine protection.

What's the difference between synthetic and conventional oil?

Synthetic oil offers better engine protection, especially in extreme temperatures, and lasts longer between changes. Most European vehicles require full synthetic oil. We'll recommend the right oil for your car.

Do you offer other fluid services besides oil changes?

Yes — we handle coolant flush, transmission fluid service, power steering fluid, brake fluid flush, and differential fluid. Regular fluid maintenance prevents costly repairs.

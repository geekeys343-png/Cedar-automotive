Expert Engine Diagnostics in Cedar Rapids

When your check engine light comes on, you need answers — not guesswork. At Cedar Automotive, we use professional-grade diagnostic scanners to read fault codes, analyze live sensor data, and pinpoint the exact cause of your engine trouble. Whether it's a misfire, rough idle, overheating, or loss of power, we identify the issue quickly so you're not paying for parts you don't need.

Our shop handles everything from basic engine tune-ups to complete overhauls on all makes and models. We also carry specialized diagnostic equipment for European vehicles like BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, and Volvo — the same tools dealerships use, without the dealership price tag.

Located in Cedar Rapids near the regional airport, Cedar Automotive is your go-to shop for engine diagnostics that are thorough, honest, and fast. Most diagnostic appointments are completed the same day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an engine diagnostic take?

Most engine diagnostic appointments at Cedar Automotive are completed the same day. A standard diagnostic scan typically takes 30-60 minutes, though complex issues may require additional testing time.

What does a check engine light mean?

A check engine light can indicate anything from a loose gas cap to a serious engine problem. Our professional-grade diagnostic scanners read manufacturer-specific fault codes to pinpoint the exact issue — not just generic OBD-II codes.

Do you work on European car engines?

Yes! We carry specialized diagnostic equipment for BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, Volvo, Porsche, and VW — the same dealer-level tools — without the dealership price tag.

Used Car Inspections in Cedar Rapids

Buying a used car is one of the biggest purchases most people make — and a bad one can cost you thousands in hidden repairs. Before you sign on the dotted line, bring the vehicle to Cedar Automotive for a thorough pre-purchase inspection. We'll check it bumper to bumper and give you an honest report of its condition, so you can buy with confidence.

Our inspection covers everything: engine condition, transmission performance, brakes, suspension, electrical systems, fluid condition, tire wear, and a visual check for signs of previous collision damage or rust. We also scan for diagnostic trouble codes that may not trigger a warning light but indicate developing problems.

This is especially important if you're looking at a European vehicle. BMWs, Audis, and Mercedes can be great cars, but they can also carry expensive hidden issues that only show up with the right diagnostic equipment. We have the tools to check European vehicles thoroughly — giving you the full picture before you commit.

What Our Inspection Covers

  • Engine condition and diagnostic code scan
  • Transmission shift quality and fluid condition
  • Brake system inspection and measurement
  • Suspension and steering component check
  • Electrical system and battery test
  • All fluid levels and condition analysis
  • Tire condition and tread depth measurement
  • Visual body and frame inspection for damage or rust
  • AC and heating system function test
  • Road test for driveability assessment

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a pre-purchase inspection include?

We inspect the engine, transmission, brakes, suspension, electrical system, fluids, tires, and body condition. We also run a diagnostic scan for stored fault codes. You get a detailed report of everything we find.

How long does a pre-purchase inspection take?

A thorough pre-purchase inspection typically takes 1-2 hours. We recommend scheduling in advance so we can give the vehicle the attention it deserves.

Can you inspect European vehicles before I buy one?

Absolutely — this is one of our specialties. European vehicles like BMW, Audi, and Mercedes can have expensive hidden issues. Our specialized diagnostic equipment catches problems that generic inspections miss.

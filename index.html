Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles does Cedar Automotive service?

We service all makes and models, including domestic, Asian, and European vehicles. We carry specialized diagnostic equipment for BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, Volvo, Porsche, and VW — the same tools dealerships use.

Do I need an appointment for auto repair?

We recommend calling ahead at (319) 540-7584 to schedule, but same-day appointments are often available. Walk-ins are welcome when our schedule allows.

Can I drop off my car before a flight at Cedar Rapids Airport?

Yes! We are located near Cedar Rapids Regional Airport (CID). You can drop your car off before your flight and pick it up repaired when you return. Call us to arrange a drop-off.

Do you provide free estimates?

We provide honest, upfront estimates before any work begins. Diagnostic fees may apply for complex issues, but we always explain costs before proceeding so there are no surprises on your bill.

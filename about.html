About Cedar Automotive

Real mechanics. Real expertise. Honest pricing.

Who We Are

Cedar Automotive is an independent auto repair shop serving Cedar Rapids, Iowa and the surrounding Eastern Iowa community. We work on all makes and models — and we're one of the few independent shops in the area equipped to handle European vehicles like BMW, Audi, Mercedes, and Volvo.

We started Cedar Automotive because we believe drivers in Cedar Rapids deserve quality car care without dealership pricing or the runaround. When you call us, you talk to a real person. When you drop off your car, you get an honest assessment and fair pricing. No upselling, no surprises.

Located at 1407 Adair Ct SW Suite 101, near the Cedar Rapids Regional Airport, our shop is easy to find and even easier to work with. Heading out on a trip? Drop your car off before your flight and pick it up fixed when you return.

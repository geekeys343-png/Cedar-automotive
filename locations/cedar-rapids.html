Cedar Rapids' Trusted Auto Repair Shop

Cedar Automotive is a locally owned and operated auto repair shop serving Cedar Rapids and the surrounding Eastern Iowa communities. Located at 1407 Adair Ct SW on the southwest side of the city near the Eastern Iowa Airport (CID), we provide full-service auto repair for all makes and models — from everyday domestic and Asian vehicles to European brands like BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, Volkswagen, Volvo, and Porsche. Whether you need a routine oil change or a complex engine rebuild, our shop has the tools, training, and experience to get the job done right the first time.

We proudly serve every Cedar Rapids neighborhood — from the SW side and Czech Village to NewBo, Marion, Hiawatha, and everything along the I-380 corridor. Our convenient location makes it easy to drop off your vehicle on your way to work or the airport and pick it up the same day. We know Cedar Rapids drivers depend on their vehicles through harsh Iowa winters and hot summers, and we keep them road-ready year-round with honest diagnostics, quality parts, and fair pricing.

At Cedar Automotive, we believe in transparent communication and no-surprise estimates. We explain what your vehicle needs, what it does not need, and let you make the decision. That straightforward approach is why Cedar Rapids families and businesses trust us with their cars, trucks, and SUVs. From engine diagnostics and brake repair to European vehicle service and car key programming, we are a one-stop shop for everything your vehicle needs.

Our Services in Cedar Rapids

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light on? We use professional-grade scanners to read factory codes and pinpoint the root cause — not just clear codes and hope for the best. From misfires and sensor failures to timing chain and turbo issues, we diagnose and repair it all.

Brake Service & Repair

Squealing, grinding, or a soft pedal? We inspect your entire brake system — pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and fluid — and replace only what is needed. We service standard and performance braking systems on all vehicle makes and models.

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes with quality filters. We also service transmission fluid, coolant, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and differential fluid to keep your vehicle running at peak performance.

Transmission Service

Slipping gears, harsh shifts, or transmission warning lights? We diagnose and service automatic and manual transmissions including fluid flushes, solenoid replacement, valve body repair, and complete rebuilds when necessary.

Electrical System Diagnostics

Modern vehicles rely on dozens of electronic modules and sensors. We diagnose parasitic battery drains, alternator failures, wiring faults, module communication errors, and infotainment system issues using factory-level scan tools.

Car Key & Fob Programming

Lost your keys or need a spare? We program transponder keys, proximity key fobs, and smart keys for most vehicle makes and models — including all-keys-lost situations. Save significantly compared to dealership pricing.

Can I drive with a check engine light on from Fairfax to your shop?

If the check engine light is steady (not flashing), it is generally safe to drive to our shop. Why is my check engine light on?

Common causes include a faulty oxygen sensor, catalytic converter issues, loose gas cap, mass airflow sensor failure, spark plug or ignition coil problems, and vacuum leaks. Iowa's temperature extremes can trigger sensor-related codes. The only way to know for sure is a proper diagnostic scan — bring it to Cedar Automotive and we will find the real answer.

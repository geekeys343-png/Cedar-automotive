Services for Solon Drivers

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light on? We use factory-grade scan tools to pinpoint the exact cause of engine issues, from misfires and sensor failures to vacuum leaks and fuel system problems. Accurate diagnostics save you time and money on repairs.

Brake Service & Repair

Squealing, grinding, or a soft brake pedal all demand immediate attention. We service brake pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and ABS systems for all makes and models. Electronic parking brake resets included for European vehicles.

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Regular oil changes are the single best thing you can do for your engine. We offer full-synthetic, synthetic blend, and conventional oil changes along with coolant flushes, power steering fluid, and differential fluid services.

Electrical System Diagnostics

Modern vehicles rely on dozens of interconnected control modules. We diagnose parasitic battery drains, alternator failures, wiring faults, and module communication errors using dealer-level equipment that reads manufacturer-specific codes.

Lost your keys or need a spare? We program car keys, key fobs, and proximity keys for most makes and models. We handle transponder key cutting, fob replacement, and all-keys-lost situations without the dealership markup.

Preventive Maintenance

Stay ahead of expensive repairs with factory-scheduled maintenance. We perform multi-point inspections, fluid replacements, belt and hose checks, filter changes, and everything your owner's manual recommends to keep your vehicle running reliably.

Our Services Near Iowa City

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light on? We use professional-grade scanners to read factory codes and pinpoint the root cause — not just clear codes and hope for the best. From misfires and sensor failures to timing chain and turbo issues, we diagnose and repair it all.

Brake Service & Repair

Squealing, grinding, or a soft pedal? We inspect your entire brake system — pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and fluid — and replace only what is needed. We service standard and performance braking systems on all vehicle makes and models.

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes with quality filters. We also service transmission fluid, coolant, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and differential fluid to keep your vehicle running at peak performance.

Transmission Service

Slipping gears, harsh shifts, or transmission warning lights? We diagnose and service automatic and manual transmissions including fluid flushes, solenoid replacement, valve body repair, and complete rebuilds when necessary.

Electrical System Diagnostics

Modern vehicles rely on dozens of electronic modules and sensors. We diagnose parasitic battery drains, alternator failures, wiring faults, module communication errors, and infotainment system issues using factory-level scan tools.

Car Key & Fob Programming

Lost your keys or need a spare? We program transponder keys, proximity key fobs, and smart keys for most vehicle makes and models — including all-keys-lost situations. Save significantly compared to dealership pricing.

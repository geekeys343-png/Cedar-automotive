## PRIORITY 1 — Do These First (Biggest SEO Impact)

### ✅ Google Business Profile — DONE

### 1. Apple Business Connect (Apple Maps)
- **URL**: https://businessconnect.apple.com
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~15 min + phone verification
- **What you need**: Apple ID, business phone available for verification call
- **Why it matters**: Controls your listing on Apple Maps, Siri, Safari, and CarPlay (your European car customers use CarPlay!)

### 2. Bing Places for Business
- **URL**: https://www.bingplaces.com
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~10 min
- **Pro tip**: You can IMPORT your Google Business Profile directly — saves tons of time
- **Why it matters**: Bing powers Cortana, Microsoft Edge, and has ~6% search market share

### 3. Yelp
- **URL**: https://biz.yelp.com
- **Cost**: Free (they'll try to sell you ads — decline)
- **Time**: ~15 min + phone verification
- **Why it matters**: Yelp listings rank on page 1 of Google for many local searches. Strong domain authority.

### 4. Facebook Business Page
- **URL**: https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~20 min
- **Category**: "Automotive Repair Shop"
- **Why it matters**: Facebook is a major local directory. Reviews here also matter. Enables customer messaging.

### 5. Foursquare
- **URL**: https://business.foursquare.com
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~10 min
- **Why it matters**: Foursquare data feeds Apple Maps, Uber, Snapchat, Samsung, and hundreds of other apps. Outsized impact.

### 6. Data Axle (Express Update)
- **URL**: https://www.expressupdate.com
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~10 min + phone verification
- **Why it matters**: Major data aggregator — your info here cascades to hundreds of smaller directories automatically

### 7. Neustar Localeze
- **URL**: https://www.neustarlocaleze.biz/directory
- **Cost**: Free
- **Time**: ~10 min + phone verification
- **Why it matters**: Another major data aggregator feeding data to search engines, apps, and directories

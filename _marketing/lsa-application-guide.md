## What is LSA / Google Guaranteed?

Local Services Ads appear at the VERY TOP of Google search results — above regular ads and the map pack. You get a green "Google Guaranteed" checkmark badge. You only pay per lead (phone call or message), not per click. Typical cost: $15-$40 per lead for auto repair.

---

## Requirements

1. **Business license** — Iowa business registration
2. **Insurance** — General liability insurance (proof required)
3. **Background check** — Google runs a background check on the business owner
4. **Google Business Profile** — Must be verified (DONE)
5. **Reviews** — Helps with ranking but not required to start

## Optimization Tips

1. **Answer every call** — Missed calls still cost money and hurt your ranking
2. **Respond to messages within 5 minutes** — Response time affects LSA ranking
3. **Ask every LSA customer for a Google review** — More reviews = higher LSA ranking
4. **Dispute bad leads** — If you get spam calls or out-of-area inquiries, dispute them for a refund
5. **Keep your GBP updated** — LSA pulls hours, photos, and reviews from GBP

## LSA vs Google Ads

| Feature | LSA | Google Ads |
|---------|-----|------------|
| Position | Top of page (above ads) | Below LSA |
| Payment | Per lead ($15-40) | Per click ($3-8) |
| Trust badge | Google Guaranteed checkmark | None |
| Control | Limited (Google picks when to show) | Full keyword control |
| Best for | Phone calls, simple services | Specific services, retargeting |

**Recommendation:** Run LSA first. If budget allows, add Google Ads Campaign 1 (Emergency) alongside LSA after 30 days.

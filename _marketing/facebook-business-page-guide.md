## Ongoing Posting Schedule

- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week
- **Content mix:**
  - Car care tips (40%)
  - Service promotions / specials (20%)
  - Behind-the-scenes / team photos (20%)
  - Customer shoutouts / reviews (when you have them) (20%)

---

## Review Strategy on Facebook
1. After every completed job, ask the customer: "Would you mind leaving us a review on Facebook or Google?"
2. Send follow-up text/email with direct link to review page
3. Respond to EVERY review — positive and negative
4. Share positive reviews as posts (with permission)

---

## Facebook Ads (Later — After Organic Foundation)
Don't spend money on Facebook ads until:
- Page has 50+ followers
- At least 5 reviews
- Regular posting schedule established (2-3x/week for 30 days)
- Then consider $5-10/day boost on best-performing posts

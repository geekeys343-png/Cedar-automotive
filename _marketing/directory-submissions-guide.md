### 1. Google Business Profile
- **Status:** DONE (verified and live)
- **URL:** https://business.google.com
- **Action:** Add 50+ photos, post weekly updates, respond to all reviews

### 2. Yelp
- **URL:** https://biz.yelp.com
- **Steps:**
  1. Go to biz.yelp.com > "Manage my free page"
  2. Search for "Cedar Automotive Cedar Rapids" — claim if exists, or add new
  3. Enter all NAP info exactly as above
  4. Add business description, hours, services, payment methods
  5. Upload 5-10 photos (shop exterior, interior, equipment, team)
  6. Verify via phone call
- **Tips:** Do NOT pay for Yelp ads initially. Focus on the free listing.

### 3. Apple Maps (Apple Business Connect)
- **URL:** https://businessconnect.apple.com
- **Steps:**
  1. Sign in with Apple ID
  2. Search for "Cedar Automotive" or add new business
  3. Enter all NAP info
  4. Add business hours, category, photos
  5. Verify (usually via phone)
- **Why it matters:** Powers Siri, Apple Maps, and Safari suggestions

### 4. Bing Places
- **URL:** https://www.bingplaces.com
- **Steps:**
  1. Sign in with Microsoft account
  2. Import from Google Business Profile (easiest) or add manually
  3. Verify all info matches GBP exactly
  4. Add photos, services, description
- **Why it matters:** Powers Bing, Cortana, Edge browser results

### 5. Facebook Business Page
- **URL:** https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
- **Steps:**
  1. Create page as "Local Business" > "Automotive Repair Shop"
  2. Enter all NAP info — must match GBP exactly
  3. Add description, hours, services, phone, website
  4. Upload cover photo and profile photo (use logo)
  5. Add all service area cities
  6. Enable Messenger for customer inquiries
  7. Post first update (e.g., "We're now on Facebook! Follow us for car care tips and specials.")
- **Why it matters:** 2nd largest local search engine, powers social proof
